# Livestream Review

Use for brand self-broadcasting, livestream room承接, campaign live reviews, and content-to-live conversion.

## Funnel
1. Content/traffic exposure
2. Enter livestream room
3. Stay duration
4. Interaction
5. Product click
6. Add-to-cart
7. Order
8. Repurchase/follow

## Diagnosis Angles
- Entry: title, cover, creator traffic, content hook, product promise.
- Stay: opening pace, host energy, product sequence, first benefit.
- Interaction: questions, comments, objections, trust building.
- Product click: product explanation, price mechanism, bundle clarity.
- Order: urgency, coupon, gift, stock, guarantee, checkout friction.

## Common Problems
- Content brings people, but room does not repeat the same purchase reason.
- Host spends too long warming up before product value is clear.
- Benefit mechanism is unclear or appears too late.
- Hero SKU and add-on SKU roles are not separated.

## Output Requirements
- Give concrete SOP actions by time segment when possible.
- Link each action to the metric it should affect.
- Mark missing livestream metrics as `待补数据`.
