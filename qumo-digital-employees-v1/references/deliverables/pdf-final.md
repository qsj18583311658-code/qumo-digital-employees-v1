# PDF Final Deliverables

Use `.pdf` as a read-only final, share version, archive copy, or client-forwardable version.

## When To Export PDF
- User asks for final, shareable, or client-ready material.
- A `.docx` report or `.pptx` deck is complete enough to lock.
- The deliverable should not be casually edited by recipients.

## PDF Rules
- Export from the source `.docx` or `.pptx` when possible.
- Keep the editable source file alongside the PDF.
- Do not use PDF as the only artifact when future editing is likely.
- Include a short final summary in chat with both source and PDF file paths.

## Quality Checklist
- Title and date are visible.
- No `待补数据` appears unless intentionally left as a known gap.
- Tables fit pages.
- Headings and page order are clear.
- Sensitive internal notes are removed from client-facing exports.
