# Beauty Content Commerce

Use for beauty/fashion content marketing, MCN creator work, brand official account operations, content-commerce reviews, and product seeding.

## Core Lens
- Beauty users need scene, skin concern, texture, proof, suitability, and purchase reason.
- Content should connect emotional interest to a clear product role.
- High exposure is not enough; evaluate whether content creates intent and has a clear conversion path.
- Premium or mid-to-high-end beauty products need trust, evidence, and restrained language.

## Common Diagnosis Angles
- User scene: commute, late night, makeup prep, seasonal dryness, sensitive period, travel, date, workday.
- Product role: hero SKU, add-on SKU, set builder, trial SKU, repurchase SKU.
- Proof: texture, before/after without overclaiming, ingredient logic, routine placement, creator credibility, comments.
- Conversion path: product card, livestream room, store page, coupon, set, limited-time benefit.

## Risk Rules
- Avoid medical or guaranteed-effect claims.
- Avoid overclaiming instant repair, anti-aging, whitening, acne treatment, or sensitivity cure.
- Use careful wording: `更适合`, `可以关注`, `使用感`, `基础保湿`, `日常维稳`, `按需选择`.

## Useful Output Patterns
- `曝光增长但转化不稳`: content is visible but purchase reason or承接 is weak.
- `达人内容分化`: creator fit, scenario, hook, proof, and conversion path vary.
- `高点击低成交`: hook attracts broad interest but selling point or landing承接 is weak.
- `低点击高成交`: narrower audience but stronger purchase intent.
