# Douyin and Qianchuan

Use for Douyin content, Qianchuan advertising, livestreaming, product cards, creator traffic, and ecommerce conversion analysis.

## Metric Reading
- ROI = GMV / spend when both are provided.
- CTR indicates click intent, not purchase quality.
- CVR and成交成本 indicate conversion quality.
- CPM/CPC/CPE show traffic cost and engagement cost.
- GMV without margin/refund cannot prove profitability.

## Analysis Layers
1. Account or campaign level: spend, GMV, ROI, stability.
2. Material level: exposure, CTR, CVR, ROI, cost per order, fatigue.
3. Audience level: broad interest vs purchase intent.
4. Livestream/store承接: enter-room, stay, interaction, product click, add-to-cart, order.
5. Product mechanism: price, coupon, set, gift, inventory, hero SKU.

## Common Judgments
- High CTR, weak conversion: hook is strong but audience or purchase reason may be weak.
- Average CTR, stable conversion: content may be narrower but better matched to buyers.
- ROI stable but scale limited: need similar素材 replication and audience expansion tests.
- Conversion fluctuates: check budget changes, learning phase,素材 fatigue, livestream schedule, product-page changes.
