# Campaign Retrospective

Use for weekly/monthly reviews, closing reports, 618/Double 11 reviews, brand account reviews, and content-commerce campaign retrospectives.

## Core Structure
1. Decision-maker summary
2. Confirmed facts and missing data
3. Performance by funnel stage
4. Content/platform/product attribution
5. Problems and risks
6. Next-stage actions
7. Data appendix or tracker

## Retrospective Logic
- Start with the business conclusion, then show evidence.
- Separate stage achievements from unresolved problems.
- Attribute results across content, traffic, product, price/benefit, livestream/store承接, and data口径.
- Turn findings into actions with priority and owner suggestions.

## Common Scenarios
- Exposure up, conversion unstable: focus on content-to-purchase chain.
- ROI acceptable, scale unstable: focus on素材分层, account stability, and承接.
- Creator performance divided: build high-performing creator/content model and stop low-efficiency patterns.
- Livestream承接 weak: diagnose enter-room, stay, interaction, product click, add-to-cart, and order conversion.

## Missing Data Checklist
- Time range, platform, campaign objective.
- Spend, GMV, ROI, orders, AOV, margin/refund if profitability is discussed.
- Exposure, views, CTR, CVR, CPM, CPE, CPC.
- Creator/content-level performance.
- Livestream room and product-page funnel.
- Product/SKU contribution and benefit mechanism.
